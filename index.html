The plan

Four days, slow.

01 Fri · Arrive

Kerala → up the hill

Fly to Mumbai. Cab to Neral (stock the booze here — way cheaper). Up to Dasturi, walk in. Check in, balcony, first round.

02 Sat · Big day

Viewpoints, lake, long night.

Viewpoint loop: Louisa → Echo → One Tree Hill. Lunch by Charlotte Lake. Evening — balcony, drinks, rain on the roof. The night.

03 Sun · Recover

Bazaar + Pramod Bar.

Lazy morning. MG Road walk, lunch at Hakimi's. Afternoon to Dodhani Waterfall. Evening at Pramod Bar — the only proper bar in town.

04 Mon · Out

Wind down, fly home

Slow breakfast. Walk down to Dasturi. Cab to airport. Afternoon flight back. Done.

FYI

The drinks map.

i.

Stock at Neral (Plan A)

Multiple liquor shops at the foot of the hill. Cheap. Hotels allow drinking in rooms and balconies.

Pramod Bar (Plan B)

The only proper bar in Matheran. MG Road. Beers, spirits, cheap. One solid night.

Hotel bars (Plan C)
